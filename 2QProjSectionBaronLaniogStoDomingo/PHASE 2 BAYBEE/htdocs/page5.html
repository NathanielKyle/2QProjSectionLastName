Kabesang Tales

By: Nathaniel Kyle Sto. Domingo

After an annual survey and/or popularity competition led by a group of Grade 9 Scholars. They have concluded that Kabesang Tales, an influential and feared bandit of San Diego, is the most popular/well-liked Jose Rizal novel character of this year.

The results were followed with Ben-Zayb in second, and Simoun/Crisostomo Ibarra in third.
