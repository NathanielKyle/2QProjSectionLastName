Pisay After Nighfall...
pt. II

By: Nathaniel Kyle Sto. Domingo

"Survival is a game. Better play your cards right, no booster packs."

-Michael Craig Sing, Convicted Murderer

Freak accidents, Deaths, and many more, This is Pisay After Nightfall...

Michael Craig Sing, a Batch 2027 Grade 9-Neon student, was a particularly normal scholar. He has normal grades, and struggled with Filipino just like other scholars. However, there was one thing that sets him aside from other people...

He was a registered assassin...

One day, Sing was given an offer, 300 pesos and free co-op gulaman for a year if he can kill a man named Chopsquad Calporto. He then spent 2 days making a trap to capture him. It had to be hidden by everyone, but also simple. That was then when he decided to make it out of chairs and gravity.

July 21 2022, It was finally the day. Craig went back to the trap, made sure it worked, and at 11:00am in the morning, Calporto went to the trap and got crushed by a million chairs.

Craig had claimed his first victim.

He had been given the bounty days later. 300 pesos and free gulaman seemed like a lot for a normal person. But not for him, he was not done at all.

Hours before Calporto's assasination, he was given another offer.

"1000 pesos for Rene."

He accepted that offer, he was even ecstatic that he was given the chance to assasinate him.

His plan was simple, bring a gun and shoot Rene Adrian Espanola, the tyrannic president of 9-Neon. Then run away and enjoy life.

He had scheduled this plan for Neon's christmas party, which was in 3 months. The only time he gets to be close to Rene.

3 months later, It was the Christmas party, Rene was giving a speech to his classmates about some random topic. Craig was ready to shoot him

As the clock struck 12, he shot his shot.

Condolences.
